üéØ 6.3 Conclusion and Recommendations

High withdrawal rates stem from Isolation-Induced Cognitive Failure, not lack of ability. The SME uses the AMPLIFY framework to reduce mental load while maintaining peer connections during the critical "Dropout Window."
